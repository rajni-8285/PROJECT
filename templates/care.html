Bathing provides relaxation to the body and reduces muscle tension. Bathing also maintains bodily hygiene and alleviates discomfort during menstruation.

Green tea

Drinking green tea protects the body from oxidative stress due to antioxidants.

warm water

Drinking warm water alleviates abdominal issues and relaxes muscles. Warm water also helps keep the body hydrated.

Dark chocolate

Vitamin C ( Fresh Fruit)

Vitamin C-rich foods strengthen the immune system and provide relief from menstrual symptoms.

Iron(food)

Iron-rich foods prevent anemia and maintain energy levels. Iron deficiency can cause fatigue, weakness, and other issues.

Magnesium (Food)

Consuming magnesium-rich foods relieves abdominal and lower back pain. Magnesium deficiency can exacerbate menstrual symptoms.
