history of aayurvedic

Herbal remedies have been used for menstrual health since ancient times. In ancient civilizations such as Egypt, Greece, and China, herbs were used to alleviate menstrual cramps, bleeding, and other issues. Some prominent herbs include ashwagandha, shatavari, and cinnamon, which help mitigate menstrual problems. These herbs are still used today and provide relief from menstrual issues.
